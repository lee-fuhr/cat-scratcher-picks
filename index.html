How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, we comb through thousands of long-term owner reviews from trusted retail sites, cat forums, and professional cat behaviorist recommendations. We look for patterns: which products hold up after six months of daily use, which posts don’t wobble, and which materials actually get scratched (instead of ignored). We also note recurring complaints, tips that snap, bases that warp, sisal that sheds, and discount any product with a consistent failure mode. Our picks are validated by published expert reviews from veterinary behaviorists and certified cat trainers. We prioritize brands with a track record of responsive customer service and easily replaceable parts. If a scratcher’s reputation tanks due to a design flaw or material degradation, we drop it.
